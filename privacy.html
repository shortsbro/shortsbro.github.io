ShortsBro Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-28

ShortsBro values user privacy and aims to comply with applicable privacy laws. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be processed through the ShortsBro app and for what purposes.

1. Information Collected or Processed

ShortsBro does not provide its own sign-up or login system. As a result, the app operator does not directly collect user names, passwords, or account credentials through the app itself.

However, Google AdMob ads may be displayed in the app. Through the Google Mobile Ads SDK, certain data may be collected or shared automatically, including IP address, user interaction data, diagnostic information, and device or account identifiers.

If a user contacts the developer directly, the user's email address and the contents of the inquiry may be collected for support purposes.

2. Purposes of Processing

  • To provide advertising and measure ad performance
  • To analyze usage trends and improve app quality
  • To identify errors, respond to security issues, and prevent abuse
  • To respond to user inquiries

3. Account-Related Information

ShortsBro does not offer its own account login feature, and the app operator does not create or manage separate user accounts for the app.

4. Third-Party Services

ShortsBro may use third-party services such as Google AdMob to display advertisements. In that process, related data may be handled by Google and its partners.

For more information, please refer to the Google Privacy Policy and the Google Mobile Ads SDK data disclosure page.

5. Retention

Contact information voluntarily provided by users is kept only for as long as needed to respond to the inquiry, unless a longer retention period is required by law. Advertising and analytics-related data may be retained and processed in accordance with the policies of the relevant third-party service providers.

6. User Choices

Users can reset or delete their Android advertising ID in device settings and can adjust personalized advertising preferences through Google and Android privacy controls.
